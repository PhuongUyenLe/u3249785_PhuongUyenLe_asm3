"Alice in Wonderland" is a fantasy adventure film directed by Tim Burton, based on Lewis Carroll's classic novels. It follows the story of Alice, a young girl who falls down a rabbit hole into a fantastical world filled with peculiar characters and nonsensical events. Doctor Strange

"Doctor Strange" is a Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) film that introduces Dr. Stephen Strange, a brilliant but arrogant neurosurgeon who loses the use of his hands in a car accident. In his quest for healing, he discovers the mystical arts and trains to become a powerful sorcerer under the guidance of the Ancient One. As he delves deeper into the world of magic, Strange must confront dark forces threatening reality itself.
